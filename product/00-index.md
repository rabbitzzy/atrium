# Product — Index

The decision layer between research and implementation. These documents answer *what we decided to build and why* — distinct from `docs/` (why we made those decisions, reference material) and `impl/` (how to build specific services).

**When research changes** (`/docs/research/`), update here first — then cascade to `/impl/`.

---

- [user-stories.md](./user-stories.md) — Who does what and under what constraints. **Designer's entry point.** Personas (Student / Teacher / Parent), story map, anti-stories.
- [prd.md](./prd.md) — What we're building, scoped and prioritized. **Co-engineer and stakeholder entry point.** Feature requirements, phase gates, success metrics, anti-goals.
- [tech-spec.md](./tech-spec.md) — How it's built, with rationale. **Co-engineer deep read.** Service contracts, data model, stack decisions, performance targets.
